Our Company!

Timely Designs(est-2017) is a well known company in the world of 10KG2. We specialize in making household items with a classy modern design as well as usability, a quality often lost in products these days. At all stages of the production process we have kept environmental sustainability in mind. Our wood is native Matai and is sourced from an old shed that is about to be burnt down so as well as having great looking grain patterns it has reducing the amount of wood in the shed therefore saving the world as we know it. We designed our product in various social and computer studies lessons and did most of the assembly on sunday, the 29th of october. The coasters are sustainably made from the off cuts of the clocks. The log rounds come from a felled tree on Sam’s farm and are sanded and varnished to perfection. The clock mechanisms were bought online in bulk and use a quartz type mechanism. The varnish used on the log rounds is locally sourced from mitre-10 morrinsville, therefore supporting our New Zealand economy. Our expert team is here to provide the time for you, please consider buying our products. .
